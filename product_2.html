Shimres

Shimres is a radiant and uplifting fragrance that sparkles with sophistication. The top notes burst with zesty citrus, leading into a heart of delicate florals. The fragrance settles into a warm, musky base, leaving a trail of timeless elegance. Shimres is the embodiment of subtle allure and graceful charm

About

From the first spritz to the lingering trail, Shimres invites you into a world of sophistication and grace.The journey begins with a burst of invigorating freshness. Bergamot and lemon zest dance together, creating a lively and uplifting introduction. Pink pepper adds a subtle hint of spice, setting the stage for the captivating symphony that follows.

As the fragrance evolves, Shimres reveals its heart, where delicate florals take center stage. Jasmine, with its sweet and intoxicating aroma, mingles with the softness of lily of the valley and the airy notes of freesia. This harmonious blend creates a bouquet of timeless beauty.


The final act of Shimres unfolds with a warm and comforting embrace. Musk provides a subtle sensuality, while sandalwood adds depth and sophistication. Vanilla, with its sweet and creamy richness, lingers on the skin, leaving an unforgettable and enticing trail.


Shimres is more than a fragrance; it's a celebration of radiant femininity.Revel in the luminosity of Shimres and let each note tell a story of timeless beauty.

An olfactory experience with a seasoning of allure.
